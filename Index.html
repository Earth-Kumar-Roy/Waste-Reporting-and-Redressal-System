A digital initiative by Kolkata Municipality to maintain sanitation standards. Report waste issues instantly, track cleanup progress in real-time, and contribute to a healthier urban environment.

Why Cleanliness Matters?

Maintaining a clean city is vital for public health, prevention of vector-borne diseases (Dengue, Malaria), and the overall aesthetic value of our heritage city. A clean environment reduces pollution and fosters community pride.

  • Disease Prevention
  • Heritage Preservation
  • Community Hygiene

Core Technology

Portal Highlights

AI Integration

Integrated AI modules detect garbage in uploaded photos instantly. This helps to remove false statements and ensure valid reports.

Regionwise Routing

Complaints are automatically routed to specific municipal zones based on the precise geo-location of the reported site.

Worker Accountability

Workers first register, and only after approval from Admin can they perform assigned work with verified proof.

Operational Protocol

How This App Works

01

Report

Spot garbage, provide your details, location, and a supportive image. AI integration instantly validates the garbage content.

02

Track

A unique ticket is generated. Citizens can monitor the status of working and provide feedback post-resolution through the registry.

03

Resolve

Workers receive exact coordinates. Once cleaned, you receive an email notification with "After" photos and worker details.
